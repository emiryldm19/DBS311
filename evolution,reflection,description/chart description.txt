# MongoDB Charts Descriptions

This document provides descriptions and insights for the charts generated from the `seneca_students` database.

## 1. Chart: Distribution of Students by Status


### Description:
This bar chart shows the distribution of students based on their status (e.g., "active", "graduated", "dropped"). The horizontal bars represent the count of students in each status category.

### Insights:
- The chart shows that there are slightly more active students than those who have graduated or dropped out.
- The "active" status has the highest count, followed by "dropped" and "graduated".
- This distribution helps in understanding the current state of the student population in terms of their academic progress.
